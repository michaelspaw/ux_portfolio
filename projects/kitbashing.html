Kitbashing

Modular asset system for rapid 3D content creation

Role

Senior UX Designer

Timeline

2021 - 2022

Tools

Figma, Maya, Substance Designer

Overview

Kitbashing is a revolutionary modular asset system that enables artists to rapidly create complex 3D environments and objects by combining pre-made components. This system significantly reduces production time while maintaining high-quality results.

The Problem

Creating detailed 3D environments traditionally requires extensive modeling time or expensive asset libraries. Artists needed a more efficient way to build complex scenes without sacrificing quality or spending excessive time on modeling.

The Work

I designed a comprehensive kitbashing system that includes:

  • Intuitive asset browser with smart categorization
  • Real-time preview of combined assets
  • Automatic UV and material management
  • Custom asset creation tools

Boolean Modifier

Advanced 3D modeling tool for complex shape operations

Role

Senior UX Designer

Timeline

2022 - Present

Tools

Figma, Maya, 3DS Max

Overview

The Boolean Modifier is a powerful tool designed to streamline complex 3D modeling operations. It enables artists to perform boolean operations (union, difference, intersection) with unprecedented precision and control, making it an essential tool for hard-surface modeling and architectural visualization.

The Problem

Traditional boolean operations in 3D modeling software often produce unpredictable results, requiring extensive cleanup and manual adjustments. Artists needed a more reliable and intuitive solution that would maintain clean topology and provide better control over the operation results.

The Work

I led the design of a new boolean operation system that introduced several key innovations:

  • Real-time preview of operation results
  • Advanced topology preservation algorithms
  • Intuitive control over operation parameters
  • Non-destructive workflow integration
